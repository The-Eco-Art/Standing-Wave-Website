⚡ ASYMMETRIC COUPLING

An asymmetric coupling exists when one system extracts energy from another. This is not inherently pathological—predator-prey, parent-child, and teacher-student all involve asymmetry. The question is whether your system can maintain integrity within the coupling.

ASYMMETRIC AMPLITUDE

One side grows while the other depletes. Net energy flows one direction.

NODE DISPLACEMENT

Your center wobbles toward the dominant system. Your regulation serves them.

BOUNDARY STATUS

Are your boundaries fixed, or are they being rewritten by the other system?

ENTROPIC DEBT

Is your capacity degrading over time? Is each cycle more depleting than the last?
